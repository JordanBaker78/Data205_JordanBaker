# Data205_JordanBaker
## Data 205 Spring 2026
### This is my capstone project for DATA 205 at Montgomery College, titled 'Exploring NFL Statistics.'

This project will be diving into basic NFL statistics for analysis. For this Project, I will be working with datasets that contain statistics from previous NFL seasons, specifically including datasets that contain information on NFL drives, player and team 
stats, scoring data, and seasonal data. The dataset(s) named ‘Drives-(year)’ have the data for each drive of 
every NFL game for that given year. For example, the dataset ‘Drives-2025’ describes each drive from 
the most recent NFL season, including the drive number, the time of the drive, and when it took place in 
the game, the result of the drive, and more. The dataset(s) named ‘Stats-(year)’ contain the team stats for 
every team for every game for that given year. For example, the dataset ‘Stats-2024 has information such 
as total passing yards, rushing yards, touchdowns, turnovers, passer rating, and more, for the 2024 NFL 
season. The dataset(s) named ‘Scoring-(year) ' have information on each score in each game in that given 
year. For example, ‘Scoring-2023 ’ shows each score, when the score happened, the score of each team at 
the time of the score, and who scored the points. The dataset(s) named ‘Season-(year)’ describes the final 
score of each game, who was playing, and who was home or away. Each dataset is sourced from Pro 
Football Reference, scraped by NFLScraPy.

My primary focus for this project is to explore trends from season to season, with a focus on the 
last 5 seasons. The datasets I will be using will be the last 5 years of data from each of the datasets I mentioned 
above. (Each year is its own dataset, so I could be working with up to 20 total individual datasets.) In my 
analysis, I will aim to answer some questions, such as: How has the league changed in 5 years? How have 
the team stats evolved? How have individual player stats evolved? Is there any correlation between a 
change in team stats and player stats? Is there any significant difference in the data from each game across 
the last 5 seasons? Which team or teams have changed the most in the last 5 seasons? What does the 
scoring data have to say about the overall offense and defense of the teams in the league? What are the 
offensive and defensive trends over the last 5 years?
